Eddie Putera Noordin

I am Eddie Putera Noordinis a Photographer andVisual artist. I was born in Kedah but raised and educated in Kuala Lumpur. I regard myself as a sketch artist, photographer and drummer. I had never pursued a formal education in the arts but rather, I see myself as an avid arts hobbyist. To put it simply, I think it runs in my blood.
